How reliable is this process?
100% guaranteed! If you meet the English Proficiency Certificate (EPC) requirement, we will successfully process your visa. For reviews you can check under the Testimonials Tab or check our Tiktok
Can I get the job without an English Proficiency Certificate (EPC)?
NO! Without an EPC, your application will be automatically declined. If you do not have IELTS or Duolingo, you must pay for an English Proficiency Certificate (EPC). If you fail to provide any of these, you are not eligible to proceed.
Are there any additional fees besides the English Proficiency Certificate (EPC)?

How long does the entire process take?

Will I receive any assistance upon arrival?
Yes! Upon arrival, you will be picked up from the airport and provided with One year free accommodation. We also assist with orientation and settling into your new environment.
