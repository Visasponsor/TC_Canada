Can I be selected without paying for the COS & AR fee?
NO!
Payment for the Certificate of Sponsorship & Associated Rights fee is a mandatory requirement. Only candidates who have paid this fee are eligible to be selected and processed. As per embassy regulations for sponsorship files, we are not allowed to pay this fee on behalf of candidate. It must be paid by the candidate.
What is COS & AR?
COS & AR stands for Certificate of Sponsorship & Associated Rights. It is a mandatory embassy-required payment that confirms your position in the sponsorship program and covers all legal, processing, and documentation rights connected to your visa process.

While the COS & AR fee is fully refundable if you choose to withdraw from the process for any reason, it must be paid by you‚Äîthe candidate. As per embassy regulations, we are not permitted to pay on behalf of any applicant as the payment receipt is one of the documents you will take to the embassy during your visa appointment.
Is COS & AR refundable?
Yes.
The COS & AR payment is fully refundable if your application is not approved or the process does not proceed for any reason.
Do I need to write any exams?
No exams required! There is no English proficiency test or written exam.
