About Us

Established in February 2021, Talent Connect Canada is a legally registered workforce placement company committed to connecting skilled and unskilled workers with Canadian employers. To date, we have successfully placed over 3,000 candidates in industries across Canada. Candidates primarily from Nigeria, Ghana, Cameroon, Kenya, South Africa, Rwanda, Uganda, and beyond.

We specialize in bridging the gap between Canadian partner companies and international talent through a transparent and efficient recruitment process. All job placements are conducted under our visa sponsorship program, which ensures legal entry and employment in Canada.

Unlike other agencies, we do not charge for visa processing, flights, or accommodation. The only mandatory requirement for all candidates is the payment for the Certificate of Sponsorship & Associated Rights (COS & AR). This is required by the embassy and must be paid by the candidate. It covers your legal sponsorship, processing rights, and official documentation for visa process.
To recover the amount invested on each candidates relocation, we implement a monthly deduction of $375 from your salary over a period of 24 months, totaling $9,000. This structured repayment allows you to move to Canada and settle into your new job without any upfront financial burden beyond the COS & AR.

COS & AR is fully refundable if your process does not go through for any reason.
